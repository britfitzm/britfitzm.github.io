About

The inspiration for this project was my childhood. Maths came naturally to me, but most of my friends struggled. They would go to extra lessons and get tutors. They were always upset because of all the answers marked wrong, even if they got more correct answers than the previous test. With these games I hope to encourage children to practice their maths skills. I have designed the game so that instead of right and wrong, you get multiple tries to get the answer as well as hints to the answer. I hope to add more games in the future.
